Smarter-Than-Human Robot Coming for Your Job

Every year Artificial Intelligence is getting smarter; it is now threatening millions of jobs.

Could yours be next?

Ai has now been taught how to do more than anybody on the planet. It can code, watch videos, create images, cook, drive cars, teach people, grant advice, comfort others and much more besides

How many Jobs can you think of that use any of these in an everyday basis?

Scientists estimate that 1.4 billion people will lose their jobs by 2030. This will mean that you won’t be able to provide for your family, buy food, pay your bills or even pay for water. This will result in a massive percentage of the worldwide population being under the poverty line, with more people becoming homeless every year.

Would You Want That for Your Children?

Ai such as Chat GPT, Google Gemini and DeepSeek AI are making people dumber every year, causing them to have a shorter attention span as young people are using AI to do their

work and to tell them what to do. At an AI Conference with Open AI in 2025, they made a statement about how ‘AI is making new advances every year and about how it will benefit jobs and reduce the need for massive corporations with thousands of employees.’ AI companies are willing to sacrifice other people’s jobs for their own gain. Every year new graduates rely on AI even more. This makes it harder for young people to get jobs, as company owners looking to hire aren’t going to hire someone who is unable to focus, isn’t able to complete a task without getting help from AI or from a senior employee.
